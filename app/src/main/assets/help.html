Frequently Asked Question

DIGITICH Mobile application provides a Guide to all Literature sets books used in Kenya, the mobile presents videos that discuss Characters, Themes, Styles and Analysis.

Below are Frequently Asked Questions

How do I Access the Videos

Click on the Father of Nations from Home Bottom Tab, You will be prompted to Make Payment of 20 KES through MPESA.

Enter your PIN to complete the transaction. You should be able to Access the Characters, Themes, Styles and Analysis For that Book

How Long does the Subscription Last

A Subscription of 20 KES Allows you to access Characters, Themes, Styles and Analysis For that Book and Lasts for 24 Hours

Do I need to Create an Account

Yes. Once you Click of one Book from Home Bottom Tab, You will be presented with a Login Page or Create Account in order to Make Payment

Which books are currently supported

Father of Nation. Other Books will be updated Soon!. Check out on our Updates!

Which payments are supported

MPESA.

What happens when I forgot my Password

On Login Page you can Select Forgot Password and Change Your Password.
